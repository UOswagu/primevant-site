Services

Primevant focuses on SOX and IT controls outcomes: clearer control design, stronger evidence, fewer findings, and reduced disruption. Engagements are remote-first with defined milestones.

1) SOX Readiness & Remediation Sprint

Designed for first-time SOX, major technology change, audit findings, or accelerated timelines.

Typical scope

  • SOX scoping & risk assessment support
  • Control design review (ITGC + key business controls)
  • RCM / control narratives refresh
  • Walkthrough prep & evidence standards
  • Remediation roadmap & tracking

Timeline: 4–6 weeks
Fee range: $35K–$75K
Engagement: Fixed-fee sprint

2) SOX Program Optimization

Reduce cost and friction by right-sizing controls, strengthening reliance, and improving evidence quality.

Typical scope

  • Key control rationalization & overlap removal
  • ITGC optimization (access, change, operations)
  • Evidence playbook & sampling standards
  • Auditor reliance alignment (where appropriate)
  • Testing approach improvements

Timeline: 6–10 weeks
Fee range: $60K–$120K
Engagement: Fixed-fee / capped T&M

3) ITGC & Access Controls Stabilization

Strengthen the IT foundations auditors focus on most: identity, privileged access, change, incidents, and evidence practices.

Typical scope

  • JML + access provisioning controls
  • User access reviews (UAR) evidence quality
  • Privileged access governance (PAM) alignment
  • Change management & SDLC evidence
  • Incident/problem controls & ticket evidence

Timeline: 3–5 weeks
Fee range: $25K–$60K
Engagement: Fixed-fee sprint

Engagement models

Fixed-fee sprint
Best for defined outcomes and rapid execution. Includes milestones and a clear deliverable list.
Capped T&M
Best for remediation where complexity varies. Includes an agreed cap and weekly burn visibility.
Retainer
Ongoing advisory with bounded hours, response expectations, and quarterly planning.

Optional add-ons (post-stabilization)

Once the SOX/ITGC foundation is stable, Primevant can support adjacent risk priorities.

  • Cyber risk assessment & governance
  • Third-party risk support (SOC report review, control mapping)
  • AI governance and controls advisory (policy + oversight)
