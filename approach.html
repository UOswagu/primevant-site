Our approach

Primevant delivers SOX and IT risk outcomes with a simple model: align expectations, reduce ambiguity, execute fast, and leave your team with sustainable practices.

1) Assess

Confirm current state, key risks, auditor expectations, and the fastest path to defensible controls and evidence.

Outputs: scope memo, timeline, deliverables, evidence expectations

2) Align

Set clear in-scope/out-of-scope boundaries, stakeholder roles, and change control to prevent scope creep.

Outputs: kickoff plan, RAID log, weekly status cadence

3) Deliver

Execute against milestones with audit-ready documentation and practical remediation support.

Outputs: updated RCM/narratives, evidence playbook, remediation tracker

4) Sustain

Leave your team with templates, standards, and knowledge transfer so progress sticks beyond the engagement.

Outputs: operating rhythm, training notes, handoff checklist

Security & confidentiality

Primevant is remote-first and handles client information with care. Engagements use secure repositories, least-privilege access, and defined retention practices.

We can align to your security requirements and vendor onboarding process as needed.

Communication

You’ll receive a clear weekly status update with progress, risks, decisions needed, and next steps.

Default cadence: weekly status + working sessions as needed.
